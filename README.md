# Iceball

[![Modrinth](https://img.shields.io/modrinth/dt/UFkHv7EN?logo=modrinth&color=00AF5C)](https://modrinth.com/mod/iceball)

Tired of your snowballs doing absolutely nothing when they hit someone? **Iceball** adds craftable and collectible balls with unique damage and effects.

## Combat Balls

| Item | Damage | Effect | Crafting |
|------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Iceball** | 1 HP | A light projectile with no cooldown | 1 Ice Block |
| **Packed Iceball** | 5 HP | More powerful than the basic Iceball, but has a short cooldown | 1 Packed Ice Block |
| **Blue Iceball** | 10 HP | Heavy hitter for serious encounters | 1 Blue Ice Block |
| **Boomball** | - | Explodes on impact - destroys blocks and entities with ease | 4 TNT and 1 Snowball |
| **Spikeball** | 30 HP | A rare and powerful item | Drops from **Ominous Vaults** |

## Lightning Balls

- **Lightning Ball** - Deals 1 HP of damage. Charge it by placing it in a chest with a *Lighting Rod* attached. Then wait for a thundstorm.
- **Charged Lightning Ball** - Summons a lightning bolt wherever it lands.

## Bouncy Balls

- **Bouncy Ball** - Bounces off surfaces when thrown; deals 1 HP of damage.
- **Big Bouncy Ball** - Place it on the ground and ride it. It moves as fast (or faster) than a horse and can jump much higher.

## Food Balls

- **Meatball** - Food item that restores hunger slightly better than beef.
- **Cooked Meatball** - Fills your saturation bar and half of your hunger bar.
